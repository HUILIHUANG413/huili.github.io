Teaching

I have served as a Head GTA and Graduate Teaching Assistant since 2020 for two large, popular graduate courses, CSE 6242 Data & Visual Analytics and CS 7641 / CS 4641 Machine Learning, across both campus and online sections, supporting thousands of students. My work spans designing and grading homework and exams, delivering guest lectures and teaching class sessions, leading recitations and office hours, and mentoring student teams on course projects. As Head GTA, I coordinate and train the teaching staff and help shape course materials. Teaching is a one of the core parts of my work, and I am committed to clear, rigorous, and student-centered instruction.
